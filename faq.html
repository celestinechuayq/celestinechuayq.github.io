FAQ

Shipping

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Shipping times vary depending on your location, but most domestic orders are delivered within 3-5 business days. We currently do not ship internationally.

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and estimated delivery date of your package.

Product Care

BriskBuddy typically lasts for up to two years with proper care. To maintain optimal performance, simply place it in direct sunlight for a few hours every month to recharge its odor-eliminating properties.

Return and Exchange

We want you to be fully satisfied with your BriskBuddy purchase. If you're not happy with your order for any reason, please contact us within 30 days of receiving your product for a full refund or exchange. Please note that items must be returned in their original packaging and condition.

Payment Information

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and Grabpay. We also offer the option to pay securely through Applepay for added convenience.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback, our friendly customer service team is here to help! You can reach us via email at support@briskbuddy.com or through our contact form on the website. We strive to respond to all inquiries promptly within 24-48 hours.
