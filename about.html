Our Story

Our journey began with a simple desire: to create a solution that would bring freshness and comfort to every fitness-loving person. Driven by our passion for innovation and wellness, we embarked on a mission to develop deodorant bags that not only eliminate odors, but also promote a healthier living environment.

Through our dedication to research, we incorporated activated bamboo charcoal from Japan to ensure that results are not compromised. Today, we are proud to be the team behind BriskBuddy to revolutionise gym experiences.
