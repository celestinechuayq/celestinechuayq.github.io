Sustainability

With BriskBuddy, we are committed to reducing our environmental footprint and promoting eco-friendly practices at every step of our journey.

We meticulously craft our deodorizer bags from consciously-sourced materials, ensuring every step aligns with our eco-friendly ethos. Our products are cruelty-free, with activated bamboo charcoal sourced from Japan, known for its natural effectiveness in odor absorption.

Our deodorizer bags come in zero-waste, 100% recyclable packaging, reducing your environmental impact by up to 70% compared to single-use packaging options.
